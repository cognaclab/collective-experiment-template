# Welcome to the Networked Prisoner's Dilemma Experiment

Thank you for participating in this study!

## Overview

In this experiment, you will be part of a group of **8 players** who will interact over **30 rounds** of decision-making.

In each round, you will be paired with another player from your group and face a strategic choice. Your decisions will affect both your own earnings and those of your partner.

## What to Expect

- **Duration**: Approximately 30-40 minutes
- **Rounds**: 30 decision rounds
- **Earnings**: You will earn points based on your decisions, which convert to real money
- **Interaction**: You will play with different partners throughout the game
- **Network**: You can choose to continue or stop playing with certain partners

## Important Notes

- Please stay focused throughout the entire experiment
- You will have limited time to make decisions in each round
- Your choices matter and affect your final payment
- All interactions are anonymous - players are identified only by numbers

Click **Continue** when you're ready to learn the rules.
