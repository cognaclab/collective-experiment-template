# Prisoner's Dilemma

## What is this experiment about?

You will be **paired with another participant** to play a simple decision-making game. In each round, you and your partner will both make a choice between two options:

- **Cooperate**
- **Defect**

## How does scoring work?

Your points depend on **both your choice and your partner's choice**:

**Both Cooperate:** You get **3 points**, Partner gets **3 points**

**You Cooperate, Partner Defects:** You get **0 points**, Partner gets **5 points**

**You Defect, Partner Cooperates:** You get **5 points**, Partner gets **0 points**

**Both Defect:** You get **1 point**, Partner gets **1 point**

## Important Details

- You will play **3 rounds** with the same partner
- Both players choose **simultaneously** - you won't know your partner's choice until after you've both decided
- After each round, you'll see what you and your partner chose, and how many points you each earned
- Your **total points** across all rounds will be converted into a **payment bonus**

## Payment

- You will receive a **base payment** of £0.50 for participating
- You will earn **1 penny per point** you accumulate during the game
- You will receive a **completion bonus** of £0.25 if you finish all 3 rounds
- Maximum possible earnings: **£0.90** (if you earn 15 points)
